About Us

Welcome to our football club! We are a group of passionate footballers who love nothing more than kicking a ball around and spending time with friends. We cater for all abilities and levels of experience, so whether you're a complete novice or a seasoned pro, you'll feel right at home here. We train twice a week and play matches on Saturdays, so there's plenty of opportunity to get involved. We also have a social side to the club, so you can come along to our events even if you're not interested in playing football. We hope you enjoy your time with us!
